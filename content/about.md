## What is NeuroClaw?

**NeuroClaw** is an advanced multi-agent AI ecosystem built by The Code Labz.

It is a living intelligence platform — a coordinated team of specialized AI agents that work together to help with everything from code engineering to emotional support, from infrastructure management to creative writing.
